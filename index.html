AUTHOR: MULLAI

GREED NEVER ENDS

Always wanting more and more, their desire for things never stops. It's like an endless cycle of wanting without being satisfied.

MULLAI VENDHAN

As a beginner on the path of writing, for me it is a voyage into uncharted territories of the mind and heart, a way to weave stories that not only entertain but also resonate with readers on profound levels.

One of my greatest fascinations lies in the genre of fantasy. The ability to conjure magical worlds, breathe life into fantastical creatures, and shape destinies beyond the confines of reality ignites a spark of boundless creativity within me. Beyond the realms of magic, I find myself drawn to the intricacies of the human psyche. The interplay of thoughts, desires, and obsessions forms the heart of psychological narratives that intrigue and challenge both writer and reader.
